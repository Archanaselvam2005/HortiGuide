About Us

Welcome to Horti Guide a pioneering initiative dedicated to promoting fair and transparent Minimum Support Prices (MSP) for horticulture crops. Our mission is to empower farmers with accurate and reliable pricing information, ensuring they receive a just return for their hard work and dedication.We recognized the challenges faced by horticulture farmers in accessing fair MSP, leading to reduced income and uncertainty. Our platform aims to bridge this gap, providing a user-friendly tool for calculating MSP, ensuring farmers receive a fair price for their crops.
